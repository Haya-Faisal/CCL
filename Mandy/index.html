Meet Mandy!

Scientific Name:

Mandy

Discovered by / at:

Haya Faisal, August 2024

Family:

Mandy does not have any immediate family, however somewhere in other puddles there might be some creatures that can be classified as his family.

Habitat:

Puddles in rainforest.

Appearance:

Mandy's body is made out of circles, with its head being the largest circle of diameter 10 and its tail being a very small circle with diameter 2. Since it is always moving in its habitat, it looks like a circle with diameters ranging between 2-10 appearing and disappearing.

Key Characteristics:

  • It is happy when in a puddle and sad when out of it.
  • It swims fast when happy.
  • It swims very slow when sad.
  • ...

What we know so far about Mandy? Mandy is a very mysterious creature and has just been found. It is also a very introverted, shy creature, with a very small lifespan when it is without a puddle. All we know about Mandy is that it lives in a puddle.It's puddle can dry up due to rising temperatures. If it is without a puddle it needs to be fed food or needs to be bought to a new puddle. If it is not fed food or is without a puddle for 2 mins, it will die.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature: When Mandy loses its puddle, please feed him food or drag the water droplet to it. If Mandy is not fed food for 30 seconds or is without a puddle for 2 mins, it will die.
