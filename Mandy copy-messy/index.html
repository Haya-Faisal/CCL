Family:

Mandy does not have any immediate family, however somewhere in other puddles there might be some creatures that can be classified as his family.

Habitat:

Puddles in rainforest

Appearance:

Mandys body is made out of circles, with its head being the largest circle of diameter 10 and its tail being a very small circle with diameter 2. Since it is always moving in its habitat, it looks like a circle with diameters ranging between 2-10 appearing and disappearing.

Key Characteristics:

  • It is happy when in a puddle and sad when out of it.
  • swims fast when happy.
  • swims very slow when sad.
